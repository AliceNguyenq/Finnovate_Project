# Finnovate_Project

Teams will be provided with anonymized datasets, presenting an individual or business
customer:
Demographics: age, gender, occupation, income bracket, location
Banking relationship: account type, balance, tenure with bank, number of active
products
Transaction behavior: transaction volume, deposit frequency, withdrawal frequency,
digital banking usage frequency
Credit profile: loan amount, loan type, interest rate, repayment history, credit score
band
Product usage: card ownership, investment products, insurance, foreign exchange
services
Customer engagement: response to past marketing campaigns, service preference
